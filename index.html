iobio

IOBIO will enable real-time interactive, visually driven analysis of genomic data. The system and the apps are under construction, but the demo apps below will give you a taste of what's coming.
Demos to collect and display important global statistics of a large genomic dataset (e.g. sequence alignments, variant call sets) in a few seconds:
1) Bam.iobio.io - take a quick look at bam files. Think google docs!
2) Variant Sampler - ipad app to see variant data statisics in realtime
Demos to analyze a specific region within a large genomic dataset, in a visually driven manner, interactively:
1) Population Clustering - cluster 1000G populations based on variants
2) Variant Analyzer - call variants in realtime form two variant callers and compare results
